Purpose

Diagnosing medical conditions is a big part of fixing problems with one's health. With the advent of data science and machine learning, it is increasingly possible to put together models that will quickly give an answer. This website is our attempt at creating models to...

  1. 1. Diagnose a medical condition when given a set of symptoms.
  2. 2. Predict an individual's risk of having a stroke, developing Hepatitis-C or Cirrhosis, or experiencing heart failure.

Operation

Use one of the options below to direct yourself to the proper prediction engine. Once there, please fill out the requested engine and hit 'Diagnose'.

Diagnosis Engine

Use this option if you want a set of symptoms diagnosed.
Risk Assessment Engine

Use this option if you want to see your likelihood of developing certain conditions.
